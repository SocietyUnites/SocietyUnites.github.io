Community Standards

This page shows our Community Standards for Mystic Games LLC, and its groups, that are and may not be limited to: "Society Unites, etc."

We’ve created these Community Standards to help ensure that everyone feels safe and welcome in our shared adventure. These Standards apply to all of your actions on Society Unites and with other Users. We want our community to be a place where people can connect with optimism and civility, regardless of their identity or beliefs.

  • Discrimination and prejudice, including homophobia, transphobia, nonaffirmation, and racism, will not be tolerated. We're an inclusive community. (This inclues using accessive misgendering, with pronouns.)
  • Offensive language and hate speech are strictly forbidden. Be respectful and considerate in your interactions.
  • Harassment and bullying are not allowed. Everyone deserves respect.
  • Respect the privacy and boundaries of others. Don't share personal information without consent.
  • Unfair advantages, cheats, and hacks are not permitted. We value fair play.
  • Report any violations to our moderation team for prompt action.
  • Violations may lead to warnings, mutes, suspensions, or permanent bans as needed.
  • We're committed to enhancing our community. Your feedback is essential.

    • By joining Society Unites, you agree to follow these Community Standards and help create a respectful and inclusive gaming environment for all. Thank you for being a part of our community.

    Mystic Games LLC has a zero-tolerance policy for endangering minors, including:

  • Any predatory behavior, including attempting to befriend a minor in order to manipulate and exploit them (i.e., grooming)
    • Sexualizing minors in any way
  • Engaging in sexual conversation with or soliciting sexual material from minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or discussing child sexual exploitation material

    • Doing any of the above will result in a imedelete ban from all services from Mystic Games LLC.

    Mystic Games LLC has a strict zero-tolerance policy against terrorism and violent extremism in any form. Any involvement or promotion of such activities is strictly prohibited.
  • The creation, sharing, or dissemination of any content that promotes violence, terrorism, or extremist ideologies is strictly prohibited on company platforms and resources.
  • Mystic Games LLC will cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and authorities in investigating and countering terrorism and violent extremism.
  • Violation of these rules will result in disciplinary action, which may include termination of employment, legal action, and reporting to the relevant authorities.

    • To maintain privacy and safety, users may be prohibited from sharing their own or requesting others’ personal information and unique identifiers, including

  • Passwords or access tokens
  • Home address or physical location
  • Financial information including credit cards and bank account details
  • Medical information
  • Telephone number
  • Unique Identifiers such as social security number
  • Any personal identifiable information relating to minors including unauthorized images or voice and video recordings.
  • Using bots that are programmed to run disruptive tasks
  • Injecting excessive loudness, screaming, or harsh noises that disrupts others’ experience, either in uploaded audio or through chat with voice
  • Falsely reporting other users or experiences or encouraging others to do so
  • Invading or flooding chats or groups in an effort to disrupt the experience or its reputation

    • These Community Standards were inspired by other games Community Standards, and OpenAI's ChatGPT. These may change with or without notice.
